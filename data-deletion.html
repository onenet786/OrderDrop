Data Deletion Request

At ServeNow, we value your privacy. You can request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data using one of the following methods.

Method 1: In-App (Fastest) Log in to the ServeNow mobile app, go to your Profile/Home screen, and tap the Delete Account (trash) icon.
Method 2: Web Request Submit the form below, and our support team will process your request within 48-72 hours.

Note: Deleting your account is permanent. You will lose access to your order history, wallet balance, and any active subscriptions.
